What is the Seltzer Club?

Origins and Mission

SUNY New Paltz is a diverse campus with many student activities taking place on a regular basis. Students are constantly using the dining hall facilities to get their fuel so they can work hard and play harder.

However, it has come to my attention that a majority of the students are drinking unhealthy bevarages from the new soda machines on campus. This makes me very sad and it just shows how people just don't like to drink water anymore.

After years of research, I have come to a reasonable solution to this unhealthy bevarage-consuming epidemic. What if students partook in a fresh, cold, spicy cup of seltzer which provides the fizz of soda and the nutrition of water?

I strive to encourage this seltzer lifestyle and hope that more people are interested in joining the movement for a seltzer-rich campus.

Like the bubbles within a nice cup of seltzer, I hope to see all of you rise to the top and join the seltzer lifestlye!
